Obtaining Equipment
btl_manual_022
Weapons and armor can be bought in <color=striking>stores</color> and become effective when you <color=striking>equip</color> them.
When Ichiban is <color=striking>Freelancer</color>, he can pull out these weapons during battle.

Check the map to find stores and see what kind of weapons and armor they sell by placing the cursor on them and pressing <symbol=button_shikaku>.
