The Art of Perfect Guarding
btl_manual_004
Guarding just before an attack hits will result in a <color=sanpo>Perfect Guard</color>, dealing no damage.
You can still perform a <color=sanpo>Perfect Guard</color> while you are in pain.
Mastering <color=sanpo>Perfect Guard</color> is the recipe to avoiding damage and breaking out of any stunlocks you may find yourself in!
